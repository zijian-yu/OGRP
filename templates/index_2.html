    Overview

    Welcome to FGBP

    FGBP (Fabaceae Genome Bioinformatics Platform) is our user-friendly, web-based comparative genomics platform based on -- published genomes of 57 Fabaceae species.The FGBP platform stores 1825 dotplots result, multilevel covariates, karyotype evolutionary trajectory animations, gene-wide structural domain identification and replication event-related genes. Analysis results for each Fabaceae family and their relationships can be displayed, searched and downloaded from the database.
