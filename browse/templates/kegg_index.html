KEGG annotations

  KEGG PATHWAY (Minoru Kanehisa et al., 2017) is a collection of manually drawn pathway maps representing our knowledge of the molecular interaction, reaction and relation networks for: 1. Metabolism 2. Genetic Information Processing 3. Environmental Information Processing 4. Cellular Processes 5. Organismal Systems 6. Human Diseases 7. Drug Development.
   Pathway annotation of 4 Oleaceae genomes were performed using GhostKoala (Minoru Kanehisa et al., 2015), respectively, it is being a tool provided by the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (http://www.genome.jp/kegg).
  Select a species of the Oleaceae family and click Submit to see the results of the gene annotation for the changed species. The relevant gene ids can be obtained from the Oleaceae blast and match under the Tools module.
