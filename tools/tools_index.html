    Overview

    Welcome to FGBP

    FGBP (Fabaceae Genome Bioinformatics Platform) is our user-friendly, web-based comparative genomics platform based on -- published genomes of 57 Fabaceae species.The FGBP platform stores 1825 dotplots result, multilevel covariates, karyotype evolutionary trajectory animations, gene-wide structural domain identification and replication event-related genes. Analysis results for each Fabaceae family and their relationships can be displayed, searched and downloaded from the database. FGBP provides 18 analysis tools commonly used in comparative genomics, and using these tools and results, users can easily observe changes in genome structure and can understand the impact of individual archaea on the genome structure and function of Fabaceae. We hope that the FGBP platform will become a great resource for the study of legumes.
