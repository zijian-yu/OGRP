Gene structures analysis

  To comparison of gene structures based on synteny, we use the GSDS visualization gene structure tool to generate visual representations of gene structures, including exons, introns, untranslated regions (utr), and coding sequences (CDS). Examine gene structure to determine conservative features such as exon/intron alignment, CDS length, and UTR length. We also provide other tools for analyzing gene structure, such as MEME, Cpgfinder and Condow.
