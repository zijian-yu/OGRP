Forsythia suspensa

Forsythia suspensa is a kind of wild vegetable oil. The oil content of Forsythia suspensa seeds is 25% -33%. The seed oil contains gum and has good volatility. It is a good raw material for insulation paint industry and cosmetics, and has great development potential. The oil can be used to manufacture soap and cosmetics, insulation paint and lubricating oil. It is also rich in oleic acid and Linoleic acid that are easily absorbed and digested by the human body. The oil taste is fragrant, and it is a good edible oil after refining.

Reference: www.gardenia.net
