Infer the evolutionary trajectories

  Infer the evolutionary trajectories (ETs) of existing species chromosomes, using AK as reference. According to the previous studies, the large-scale chromosomal rearrangements (CRs) often include end-end joining (EEJ), nested chromosome fusion (NCF), reciprocal or non-reciprocal chromosome translocation (RCT or non-RCT), and intra-chromosomal inversion (CI).
