Flowering time genes

Flowering in higher plants can be divided into two stages: the transition from stem tip meristem to floral meristem and the formation of floral organs. The former is mainly controlled by flowering genes, while the latter stage is mainly caused by the regulation of plant homologous (Heterotypic) cassette genes.
